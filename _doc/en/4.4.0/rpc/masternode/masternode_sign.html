--- name: masternode_sign btcversion: 4.4.0 btcgroup: masternode permalink: en/doc/4.4.0/rpc/masternode/masternode_sign/ --- masternode_sign "msghash" Sign BLS message against previous CL + msghash using MN operator BLS key. Must be in winners list as well. Arguments: 1. msghash (string, required) msg hash.
