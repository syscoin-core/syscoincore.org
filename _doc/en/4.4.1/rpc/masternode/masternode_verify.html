--- name: masternode_verify btcversion: 4.4.1 btcgroup: masternode permalink: en/doc/4.4.1/rpc/masternode/masternode_verify/ --- masternode_verify "msghash" "signature" "blspubkey" Verify BLS message signed against previous CL + msghash using MN operator BLS key. Must be in winners list as well. Arguments: 1. msghash (string, required) msg hash 2. signature (string, required) signature 3. blspubkey (string, required) BLS MN operator pubkey Examples: > syscoin-cli masternode_verify > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id": "curltest", "method": "masternode_verify", "params": []}' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
